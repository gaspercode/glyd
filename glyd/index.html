ABOUT
US

Wouldn’t it be great to experience the best of what a place has to offer‒with someone who lives there?

That’s the simple question we asked ourselves when we created GLYD. Others may choose to visit new places, seeing only what’s at the surface‒but not us. We embrace new cultures while looking to immerse ourselves in something special. That’s just our thing.

If travel is in your DNA. If you’re passionate about connecting with interesting people and discovering new destinations around the world, then feel free to make GLYD your thing.
